Explain how to play the game

  • to go up--- you need to click on the up key

  • to go down--- you need to click on the up key

  • to go left--- you need to click on the left key

  • to go right--- you need to click on the right key

  • to shoot--- you need to click on the space key

how to win!

  • you need to push your keys and run away from the enemies

  • shoot the enemis whith your gun, if you manage to get them they will kill

  • every good shoot the score on the side of the page gives you 100 points

  • there is a gameover when you get crashed with an enemy to prevent this

  • if you got a gameover you can press at the space key to return to play
